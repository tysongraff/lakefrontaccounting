Typical Pricing

The pricing below reflects typical starting points for our services. Fees vary based on complexity, volume of activity, and specific filing requirements. We will always confirm pricing before work begins.

Business Tax Returns

  • C Corporation, S Corporation, or Partnership: $1,000
  • Additional assets (e.g., rental properties): $150 per additional asset
  • Additional state return: $150 per state
  • Section 754 step-up: $300

Individual Tax Returns

  • Base individual return: $250
  • Schedule C or Schedule E: $150 per filing
  • Schedule D: $75
  • Additional state return: $150 per state

Other Services

  • Advisory, tax planning, and other services: $200 per hour

We also offer year-round service packages that may include bookkeeping, tax planning, advisory support, and tax preparation, billed on a monthly basis. Please contact us to discuss package options.
