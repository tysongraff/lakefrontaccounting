Typical Pricing

The pricing below reflects typical starting points. Fees vary based on complexity, transaction volume, and filing requirements.

Business Tax Returns

  • C Corporation, S Corporation, or Partnership: $1,000
  • Additional assets: $150 per asset
  • Additional state return: $150 per state
  • Section 754 step-up: $300

Individual Tax Returns

  • Base individual return: $250
  • Schedule C or E: $150 per filing
  • Schedule D: $75
  • Additional state return: $150 per state

We also offer year-round service packages that may include bookkeeping, tax planning, advisory support, and tax preparation, billed monthly.
