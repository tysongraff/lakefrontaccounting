About Lakefront Accounting

Trusted advisors with real-world experience.

Lakefront Accounting is a CPA firm focused on providing thoughtful, relationship-driven accounting and tax services to individuals, small businesses, and real estate investors.

We believe accounting should go beyond compliance. Our goal is to help clients understand their numbers, plan proactively, and make confident financial decisions.

Our Background

Our firm is led by CPAs with deep experience in the real estate industry and small business advisory. We regularly work with:

  • Real estate investors and rental property owners
  • Entrepreneurs and closely held businesses
  • S-Corporations and pass-through entities
  • Individuals with complex tax situations

How We Work

We take a proactive, collaborative approach with every client. That means clear communication, timely advice, and strategies tailored to your specific situation — not one-size-fits-all solutions.

Whether you’re growing a business, building a real estate portfolio, or simply looking for a CPA you can trust, we’re here to help.
